# Monetization & Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscriptions (Membership)
*   **Monthly/Annual Plans**: Access to premium content, advanced courses, or exclusive contest categories.
*   **Pro Benefits**: Badge on profile, priority support, discounts on workshops.

### 2. Physical & Digital Products
*   **Accessories**: Merch (T-shirts, stickers), camera straps, cleaning kits.
*   **Digital Assets**: Presets (Lightroom), eBooks, Texture packs.

### 3. Education (Workshops & Talleres)
*   **Online Courses**: Pre-recorded video content.
*   **Live Workshops**: Webinars or live editing sessions.
*   **Field Trips (Salidas)**:
    *   **Studio Sessions**: Guided practice in professional studios.
    *   **Location Shoots**: Organized outings to tracks, scenic routes, or urban locations for automotive photography.
    *   *Pricing*: Ticket-based per event.

### 4. Sponsorships
*   Brand partnerships for specific contests (e.g., "Sony Photo of the Week").

## Creator Economy (User Monetization)
*   **Course Creation**: Top photographers can create and sell their own courses on the platform. Revenue share model (e.g., 70/30 split).
*   **Image Sales**:
    *   **Digital Downloads**: Users can sell high-res licenses of their work.
    *   **Prints**: Option to sell requested printed versions (platform handles logistics or connects with print partners).
