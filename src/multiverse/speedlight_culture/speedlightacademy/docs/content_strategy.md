# Content Strategy & Schedule

## Publishing Schedule
The platform aims to maintain active engagement through a defined publishing calendar.

### Monthly Cadence
*   **Photo of the Week**: 4 publications per month (1 per week).
*   **Photo of the Month**: 1 publication per month highlighting the best of the weeks.

### Annual Cadence
*   **Photo of the Year**: 1 grand publication annually.
*   **Top Edits of the Year**: 1 publication annually showcasing the best editing work.
*   **Photographer of the Year**: Recognition of top talent.
*   **Editor of the Year**: Recognition of top talent.

## User Participation
*   Users can post/upload their photos for these specific calls (Convocatorias).
*   The system needs to handle submissions and potentially voting or judging mechanisms (to be defined).
