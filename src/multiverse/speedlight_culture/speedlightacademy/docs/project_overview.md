# Speedlight Academy - Project Overview

## Vision
Speedlight Academy is a web platform designed to connect students and professors in the field of photography and editing. It serves as a learning hub where users can track their progress through courses and participate in community-driven competitions.

## Ecosystem Integration
**Speedlight Academy** is the educational pillar of the **Speedlight Culture** ecosystem. It operates in synchrony with the main platform to offer a comprehensive experience:
*   **Unified Account**: Potential for shared credentials between Culture and Academy.
*   **Synergy**: Users from Speedlight Culture are funneled to Academy for learning, and Academy graduates showcase their work (photos, builds) back on Culture.
*   **Domain**: Operates at `speedlightacademy.com` but remains visually and functionally linked to the generic Speedlight brand.

A personalized area for users to:
*   Track their career progress.

### Competitions & Community
The platform fosters engagement through regular contests where users can submit their work.

## Educational Scope & Alliances
While the core focus is **Photography & Media**, Speedlight Academy aggregates motorsport education through strategic partners:

1.  **Photography & Editing**: Core curriculum produced by Speedlight.
2.  **Mechanics**: Courses provided by **Escuela Nacional Automotriz** (Credits to partner).
3.  **Driving (Drift/Rally)**: Courses provided by **Rent for Racing** (Credits to partner).
4.  **Wrapping**: Courses provided by **Wrap Masters** (Credits to partner).
