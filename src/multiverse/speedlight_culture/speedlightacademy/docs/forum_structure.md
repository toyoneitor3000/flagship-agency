# Community Forum Structure

The Forum serves as a knowledge base and networking hub for professors and students.

## User Roles in Forum
*   **Professors & Professionals**: Consultants for techniques (2RK, etc.), equipment, and production. Verified experts.
*   **Students/Community**: Can ask questions, share tips, and organize meetups.

## Monthly Structure
*   **Monthly Dynamics**: Forum discussions and organized activities should often relate to the current month's theme or "Photo of the Month" Challenge.

## Categories / Boards
1.  **Photography Techniques**: Lighting, composition, settings.
2.  **Equipment (Consultations)**: Cameras, lenses, accessories.
3.  **Content Creation**: Reels, backstage, social media strategies.
4.  **Locations**: Sharing and reviewing spots for shoots.
5.  **Marketplace / Rentals**:
    *   Vehicle Rentals (for automotive shoots).
    *   Equipment Rentals.
    *   Studio Rentals.
6.  **Lighting**: Specific discussions on strobes, continuous light, modifiers.
7.  **Networking & Dynamics**:
    *   "Meet other photographers".
    *   Organizing impromptu photo walks or challenges.
